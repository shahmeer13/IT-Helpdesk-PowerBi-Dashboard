## Purpose of the Dashboard
The dashboard is designed to provide clear insights into helpdesk efficiency and workload management. It helps demonstrate how Power BI can be used to:

- Monitor total ticket volume
- Track open vs resolved tickets
- Identify high-priority and critical issues
- Measure resolution performance and trends

---

## Key Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- Total Tickets
- Open Tickets
- High Priority Tickets
- Resolution Rate
- Average Resolution Time

### Visual Reports
- Tickets by Priority  
- Tickets by Category  
- Tickets by Technician  
- Trend and performance insights
